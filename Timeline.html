Timeline

  • 1923

    Oct 27th, born in Manhattan in a middle-class family.

  • 1937

    Enrolls in watercolor classes at an Art School, which is Parsons The New School for Design Now.

  • 1940

    Studied at the Ohio State University for undergraduate degree, and join a fraternity.

  • 1943

    Joined the US Army, and started the training at Texas.

  • 1944

    Started training at Air Force as a pilot.

  • 1946

    Returned to the Ohio State University to complete the degree. Became a member of the Fine Art Faculty in the same year.

  • 1947

    Enrolled in the Graduate School at OSU.

  • 1951

    First solo exhibition in New York City, at Carlebach Gallery.

  • 1957

    Bought the first home in Cleveland Heights and set up a studio there.

  • 1961

    Separated with his wife and set up his studio in New York.

  • 1962

    Work was included for the first time as part of the MoMA’s Art Lending Service.

  • 1972

    Began Still Life series

  • 1973

    Began Artist’s Studio Series

  • 1979

    Made last Surrealist-inspired works.

  • 1984

    Joined board of directors of the Studio in a School Association

  • 1988

    Began Reflections series in Southampton

  • 1990

    Begins Interiors series

  • 1994

    First series of Nudes was shown at Leo Castelli.

  • 1997

    Died unexpectedly at New York Medical Center.
