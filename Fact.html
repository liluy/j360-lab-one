Facts of Roy Lichtenstein

  1. Lichtenstein created first proto-Pop Work in 1956, during the middle of his career.
  2. His first Solo Exhibition in Europe at Paris in 1963.
  3. ‘Whaam!’ is Roy Lichtenstein’s most famous painting
  4. He was teaching at Rutgers University and Ohio State University.
  5. Lichtenstein had stopped using comic book sources in his later career.
  6. He also made a film named ‘Three Landscapes’.
  7. His painting ‘Woman with Flowered Hat’ which fetched $56.1 million in May 2013
  8. Lichtenstein got married twice.
  9. He was awarded the National Medal of the Arts
  10. Lichtenstein died of complications from pneumonia.
